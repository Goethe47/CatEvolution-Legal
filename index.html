Privacy Policy — Cat Evolution

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Cat Evolution (package name com.goethe47.catevolution, developed by Goethe47, "we", "us") handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not operate our own backend servers. However, the app includes third-party SDKs that may collect and process information as described below.

  • Advertising and device data — Unity LevelPlay, Unity Ads, and the advertising services enabled for the app may process advertising and other device identifiers, personal identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, device and operating system information, app identifiers, ad impressions, ad clicks and other interactions with advertisements, app diagnostics, fraud-prevention signals, purchase-related signals where available, and app usage time where advertising optimization features are enabled.
  • Google Play Games data — Google Play Games Services may authenticate the player using their Play Games profile and provide the game with a stable player ID. The game submits the player's high score to the Google Play Games leaderboard. Cat Evolution does not use Google Play Games Saved Games or cloud saves.
  • Gameplay data — game progress, settings, and the local high score are stored on your device. Your high score may also be submitted to the Google Play Games leaderboard if Play Games authentication is available. We do not operate a server that receives or stores your gameplay progress.

3. Your Ad Choices

The first time you open Cat Evolution, you're asked whether to allow personalized ads. You can change this choice at any time from the in-app menu ("Ads & Privacy").

You can also limit ad tracking at the device level:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

Residents of certain U.S. states may have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Where applicable, these choices are available through the in-app "Ads & Privacy" screen and through the privacy controls provided by our advertising partners.

4. Data Retention

Locally stored gameplay data remains on your device until the app's data is cleared or the app is uninstalled. Depending on your Android backup settings, some local data may be backed up and restored by the operating system when you reinstall the app or set up a new device. Data held by Unity and Google is retained and deleted according to their own policies linked above.

5. Children's Privacy

Cat Evolution is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below.

6. Your Rights and Deleting Your Data

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data held about you. Cat Evolution does not maintain user accounts or server-side gameplay profiles.

  • Local game data can be deleted by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • Google Play Games data, including leaderboard data, can be managed or deleted through your Google Play Games profile or Google Account controls.
  • Requests about advertising data must be submitted through the privacy request mechanisms provided by Unity and the applicable advertising partners.

Contact us first using the email below if you need help identifying the appropriate service provider.

7. Data Security

Cat Evolution does not transmit gameplay progress to servers operated by Goethe47. Third-party services used by the game state that data transmitted through their SDKs is protected in transit. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

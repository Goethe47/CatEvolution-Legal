Privacy Policy — Cat Evolution

Last updated: July 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Cat Evolution (package name com.goethe47.catevolution, developed by Goethe47, "we", "us") handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not operate our own backend servers. However, the app includes third-party SDKs that may collect and process information as described below.

  • Age-screen information — the app asks for a date of birth in a neutral age screen. The date is processed only in memory on your device to determine whether the player is in the protected under-18 group or the adult 18-plus group. The entered date of birth is not saved, logged, or transmitted. Only the resulting age category is stored locally on the device. The category is used to select the appropriate advertising privacy configuration.
  • Advertising and device data — Unity LevelPlay and Unity Ads may process advertising and other device identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, device and operating system information, app identifiers, ad impressions, ad clicks and other interactions with advertisements, diagnostics, fraud-prevention signals, and app usage information. The exact processing is limited by the player's derived age category and advertising choices as described below.
  • Google Play Games data — Google Play Games Services may authenticate the player, process the player's gamer identity (including gamertag and avatar), provide the game with a stable player ID, and collect analytics and diagnostics needed to operate and improve the service. The game submits the player's high score to the Google Play Games leaderboard.
  • Gameplay data — game progress, settings, and the local high score are stored on your device. Your high score may also be submitted to Google Play Games if authentication is available. We do not operate a server that receives or stores your gameplay progress.

3. Neutral Age Screen and Ad Choices

Before any advertising SDK is initialized, Cat Evolution asks for a date of birth. The date is used on the device only to derive one of two advertising groups and is then discarded:

  • Protected under 18 — the personalized-ad choice is not offered. The app configures LevelPlay and Unity Ads for restricted, contextual, non-behavioral advertising, sets child-directed and non-behavioral advertising signals, configures LevelPlay to initialize only Families Self-Certified advertising SDKs for that user, and prevents the advertising SDK from accessing the Android Advertising ID for that user.
  • Adult 18 plus — the player is asked to choose personalized or non-personalized advertising. Personalized advertising is enabled only after this explicit choice.

Ads may still be shown when personalization is declined. Limited data may still be processed for contextual ad delivery, measurement, frequency capping, diagnostics, security, and fraud prevention. The in-game Ads & Privacy screen allows every player to open this policy, use the applicable sale/share opt-out, and recheck their age category. Protected players do not see controls that enable personalization.

If an age recheck crosses between the protected and adult groups, any previous personalized-ad choice is cleared. An adult must make a new advertising choice; an older choice never becomes active automatically. If advertising has already initialized when age or privacy settings change, new ad creation and loading stop. The saved settings are fully applied before SDK initialization on the next game launch.

Residents of certain U.S. states may have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. This control is available through the in-app Ads & Privacy screen. Absence of an opt-out is not recorded as an affirmative permission to sell or share data.

You can also manage Android advertising controls through Settings → Google → All services → Ads. Available options may vary by Android version and device manufacturer.

4. Data Retention

The entered date of birth is not retained. The derived age category, advertising choices, game progress, and settings remain locally on the device until they are reset, the app's storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled. Cat Evolution is configured not to participate in Android application backup and restore. Data held by Unity and Google is retained and deleted according to their own policies linked above.

5. Children's and Protected Players' Privacy

Cat Evolution is configured as a mixed-audience app. It uses a neutral age screen before advertising starts. Players classified in the protected under-18 group receive restricted, non-personalized advertising and the app sends the advertising platform signals intended to prevent use of the Android advertising identifier and behavioral advertising for those users. Players whose age category is unknown receive no advertising because the advertising SDK is not initialized until the age screen is completed.

The under-18 threshold is a conservative product protection used by Cat Evolution. It does not state that every person aged 13–17 has the same legal status under every country's law.

6. Your Rights and Deleting Your Data

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data held about you. Cat Evolution does not maintain user accounts or server-side gameplay profiles.

  • Local game, age-category, and privacy-choice data can be deleted by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • Google Play Games data, including leaderboard data, can be managed or deleted through your Google Play Games profile or Google Account controls.
  • Requests concerning advertising data may be submitted through the privacy request mechanisms provided by Unity and any applicable advertising partners.

You may contact us using the email below if you need help identifying the appropriate provider.

7. Data Security

Cat Evolution does not transmit gameplay progress or the entered date of birth to servers operated by Goethe47. Unity states that data collected through the Unity Ads SDK is encrypted in transit. Google Play Games data is processed according to Google's security and privacy practices. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

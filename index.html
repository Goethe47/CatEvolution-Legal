Privacy Policy — Cat Evolution

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Cat Evolution (package name com.goethe47.catevolution, developed by Goethe47, "we", "us") handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not operate our own backend servers. However, the app includes third-party SDKs that may collect and process information as described below.

  • Advertising identifiers & device data — when ads are shown, our advertising partner (Unity LevelPlay / Unity Ads) may collect your device's advertising ID (GAID), IP address, general (coarse) location derived from IP, device model, OS version, and ad interaction data (impressions, clicks), used to serve and measure ads.
  • Google Play Games data — Google Play Games Services may authenticate the player using their Play Games profile and provide the game with a stable player ID. The game submits the player's high score to the Google Play Games leaderboard. Cat Evolution does not use Google Play Games Saved Games or cloud saves.
  • Gameplay data — game progress, settings, and the local high score are stored on your device. Your high score may also be submitted to the Google Play Games leaderboard if Play Games authentication is available. We do not operate a server that receives or stores your gameplay progress.

3. Your Ad Choices

The first time you open Cat Evolution, you're asked whether to allow personalized ads. You can change this choice at any time from the in-app menu ("Ads & Privacy").

You can also limit ad tracking at the device level:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

California residents (CCPA/CPRA): declining personalized ads in the in-app "Ads & Privacy" screen is treated as opting out of the sale/sharing of your information for cross-context behavioral advertising. You may also submit a request using the contact email below.

4. Data Retention

Locally stored gameplay data stays on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its data. Data held by Unity and Google is retained and deleted according to their own policies linked above.

5. Children's Privacy

Cat Evolution is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below so we can remove it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data held about you. Contact us first using the email below. Where information is controlled directly by Google or Unity, we may direct you to the appropriate privacy request mechanism.
